Impact Of Technology In Our Daily Life

In this 21st century, technology has greatly affected our daily life and has a huge impact on our day-to-day life. It has been making our life easier and more convenient day by day. people nowadays are fully dependent on technology.Technology is affecting our daily life from transport efficiency and safety, to access to food and healthcare, socialization , and productivity. The impact of technology on our lives has changed the way we live and do everything. Technology has been playing a huge role in developing almost all industries such as business, education, banking, health, transportation, entertainment, etc. Online class in this pandemic has been possible by technology as well. Whether we are in the office, school, hospital, or at home the effects of technology on society are unmeasurable. We can't even imagine how hard our life be without technology. The high-tech gadgets and appliances have improved the quality of our lives and made it more convenient. Some of the technology we use mainly in our daily lives are Smartphones, laptops, Wifi-routers, LED TV, Fans, Air conditioner, geysers, Water filter, etc. Technology has made the communication between people far from each other possible. The internet has made it possible to share ideas and resources over the network. Technologies used in hospitals are used to save the lives of people. And if we don't use technology properly it may have a bad impact on us.
